Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is Hamara Apna Bazaar?

Hamara Apna Bazaar is an online marketplace where you can find a wide range of products from trusted vendors across India, delivered straight to your doorstep.

2. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our website, add the desired items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.

3. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets such as Paytm, Google Pay, and PhonePe.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this tracking number to check the status of your delivery on our website.

5. What is the return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy on most items. Please refer to our Returns and Refunds section for detailed information on how to return an item and the conditions that apply.

6. How do I contact customer support?

If you need any assistance, you can contact our customer support team via email at support@hamaraapnabazaar.com or call us at +91-12345-67890.
